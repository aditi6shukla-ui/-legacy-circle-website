How It Works

A three-part induction,
not a sales process.

The work begins before you are aware of it. Our research team maps your trajectory privately before any introduction is made.

01 — Research

We find you
before we meet you.

Our team maps your social and professional footprint quietly, before any conversation begins. We identify where your reputation stands, where the gaps are, and whether Legacy Circle can genuinely help.

02 — Introduction

You are evaluated,
not pitched to.

The first meeting is a genuine assessment. We want to understand your family's ambitions and timeline. You will be asked questions most advisors never ask. This is intentional.

03 — Induction

The work begins

Once accepted, a dedicated team begins work on a long-term refinement roadmap. 24/7 monitoring, encrypted communications, and engineered introductions to the right networks. All without noise.

What We Do

Six disciplines.
One unified mandate.

Reputation Architecture

Long-term strategy for positioning a family name or business within high-value cultural and social networks. Proactive by design, not reactive to events.

Network Engineering

Curated introductions to board positions, cultural institutions, private clubs, and elite professional circles. Done with precision, patience, and full discretion.

Digital Footprint Curation

Management of your family's and business's online presence across search and AI platforms. What the right people find when they look matters more than ever.

Cultural Positioning

Art advisory, charity board placement, and cultural association that signal taste, legacy, and philanthropic intent authentically — never performatively.

Legacy Transition

Managing the reputational dimension of generational handovers, corporate mergers, IPOs, and public milestones that demand careful, deliberate positioning.

Ongoing Intelligence

24/7 reputation monitoring with an encrypted, zero-leak communications protocol. Your team knows the moment something requires attention.
